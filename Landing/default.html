FAQs

Where is my data saved? plus_circle_fill
All your data is saved on the current device until you sync before it is uploaded in the cloud.
Where does sync mean? plus_circle_fill
When you sync, you are downloading all your data from the cloud into your current device and also uploading the data from your device to the cloud. It's a two-way data copying.
Do I need internet to use this app? plus_circle_fill
You do need internet to use it in a browser but you do not need internet for the app.
